# Narrative Engineering Prompt

Use this prompt to transform descriptive content into assertion-based headlines that drive a compelling narrative.

---

## Prompt

```
Transform this content analysis into presentation headlines.

## Content Brief
[PASTE CONTENT ANALYSIS FROM PHASE 1]

---

## Rules for Headlines

1. **Assertions, not descriptions**
   - ❌ "About Our Product"
   - ✅ "Most API Gateways Fail at Scale"

2. **Specific over general**
   - ❌ "Key Features"
   - ✅ "Three Capabilities Your Competitors Don't Have"

3. **Stakes over features**
   - ❌ "Security Features"
   - ✅ "One Breach Costs $4.5M on Average"

4. **Active over passive**
   - ❌ "Solutions Are Available"
   - ✅ "You Can Control This Today"

---

## Required Output

### Slide 1: Title
- Main title: [Big claim + topic]
- Subtitle: [Value proposition]

### Slide 2: Hook
- Headline: [Provocative shift, question, or surprising fact]
- Purpose: Capture attention, establish relevance

### Slide 3: Problem
- Headline: [Pain point with specific stakes]
- Purpose: Make the problem feel urgent

### Slide 4: False Solutions
- Headline: [What doesn't work / myths exposed]
- Purpose: Clear the field, build credibility

### Slide 5: True Solution
- Headline: [Your answer with differentiation]
- Purpose: Position your approach

### Slide 6: Architecture/How
- Headline: [How it works - visual concept]
- Purpose: Make it concrete

### Slide 7: Proof
- Headline: [Evidence-backed claim]
- Purpose: Build belief

### Slide 8: Benefits
- Headline: [Outcome-focused value]
- Purpose: Connect to audience goals

### Slide 9: CTA
- Headline: [Question that drives action]
- Answer: [Key message + next step]
- URL: [destination]

---

## Quality Check
For each headline, verify:
- [ ] It's an assertion, not a description
- [ ] A skeptic would want to see the proof
- [ ] It could stand alone as a tweet
- [ ] It advances the narrative
```

---

## Expected Output

A complete headline list for every slide, each crafted as an assertion that:
- Makes a claim
- Creates curiosity
- Drives the narrative forward
